Semester One Reflection

This semester was an impotant semester as there were so much events that were happening and unfolding. The Covid-19 outbreak had been the first ever global pandemic in a long time and it was interesting how things had to be changed around this. Also, part of this semester was remote learning which was a completely new experience for me and everyone.

In Maths, I found it was easy to manage and stayed ontop of all my tasks. I managed to stay organised and my academic scores were averaging high A's and A+'s. I found this was the easiest subject for me as most of the topics I learnt, I had previous knowledge of.

In English, I found it very easy it cope with everything and handle every one of my tasks. I was very organised and I knew what to do, how and where to do it. My teacher was also a very nice teacher which lead to me being less stressed with everything.

In Science, I learned new concepts and information as it was a whole new curriculum. I found it easy to complete my practical reports as I had many experience from last year. I averaged around 100% for most of my tasks and assignments. The experiments were completely different from last year as there were more concepts involved and new information we learned throughout the term.

Overall, the semester was amazing and certainly one that I would remember. It taught me so much things and new skills that I developed aswell.
