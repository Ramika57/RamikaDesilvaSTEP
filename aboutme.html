About Me

School|

My name is Ramika Desilva, and I am a student in the Rosehill Secondary College S.T.E.P program. The S.T.E.P program is a program for students who are academically ahead of the curriculum and have the core subject material one year ahead. Since the beginning of my school life, I have always been one or two steps ahead of the curriculum. During my time in S.T.E.P, I have created close bonds with the teachers and students.
